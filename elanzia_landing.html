Questions jewellers ask

Before you register

What does it cost to sell on Elanzia?

Registration is free. Founding sellers pay zero listing fees; a transparent commission applies only when you make a sale.

Is shipping safe for high-value jewellery?

Yes. Every shipment is picked up from your door and insured in transit for the declared value.

When do I receive my payment?

Payments are settled directly to your registered bank account within T+2 working days of delivery confirmation.

Do I need a GST number?

Yes, an active GSTIN is required to sell. Keep it handy during your onboarding call to go live faster.

Can I sell if I am not good with technology?

Absolutely. Our team handles setup, photography support, and your first product listings with you.
